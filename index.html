Flitter: Flat twitter client

Fork of the proprietary twitter client MetroTwit Loop.

What's done: 1. Full functionality of MetroTwit is kept. 2. MetroTwit Plus (Multi-account support and ads disabling) is enabled for default. 3. Little themes tidy up (Now only Dark theme supported, don't try to select Light theme)
